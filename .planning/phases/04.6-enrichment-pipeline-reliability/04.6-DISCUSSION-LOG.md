## Briefing Vision (User-Initiated)

User described their vision before gray area selection:
- Needs a daily brief they can trust: not missing important news
- Wants a learning/improvement period with user-assisted enrichment
- TLDR articles arrive at unpredictable times, get left out of daily briefing
- Re-enrichment should not rewrite past briefings
- Current model (fixed daily window) doesn't work

**User's core question:** "What happened since I last looked?" (not "What happened today?")
**Usage pattern:** Checks morning and evening

**Proposed model:** "Enrich for the next briefing" with continuous enrichment, briefing snapshots, user feedback, and bigger batches.

| Option | Description | Selected |
