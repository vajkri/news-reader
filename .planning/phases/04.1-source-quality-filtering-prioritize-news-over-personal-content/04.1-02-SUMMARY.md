## What Changed

### Enrichment SYSTEM_PROMPT (src/lib/enrich.ts)

Before: 7-line flat numbered list with vague instructions.

After: Structured with 7 named sections:
- **Summary Rules:** explicit "This" implication rule + anti-pattern list (no "In a recent post...", "The article discusses...", "According to...")
- **Importance Scoring:** 4 anchors (9-10/7-8/4-6/1-3) with concrete examples; 60/40 weighted criteria
- **Topic Classification:** 1-3 topics per article; create new only when no seed fits AND clearly recurring
- **Content Type:** 5 types with key distinction rule (announcement vs news)
- **Thin Content:** explicit 50-word threshold replacing vague "too short or generic"

### Chat SYSTEM_PROMPT (src/app/api/chat/route.ts)

Before: Flat RULES + CAPABILITIES AND LIMITATIONS blocks with negative framing.

After: Restructured with 5 named sections:
- **What You Do Well:** 4 specific capabilities named (trend analysis, company/model lookup, cross-source comparison, significance in context)
- **Rules:** answer-first rule with explicit prohibited phrases
- **Tool Selection:** maps question patterns to tool names
- **When You Cannot Help:** fast-failure one-sentence response rule; no apology padding
- **Formatting:** retained and clarified
