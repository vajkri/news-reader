The SUMMARY for Plan 05 (commit `13dff02`) claims this was added, but inspection of the actual commit confirms `button.tsx` was not modified — the commit only touched `prisma/schema.prisma`, `FeedbackButtons.tsx`, `BriefingCard.tsx`, and `index.ts`.
